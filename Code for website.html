2024 will bring in other trending colors too like royal blue or lilac purple. Throughout the progression of the year, I predict a step away from the quiet luxury style that has been in trend as of late. I see more of a classic preppy style entering the spotlight..

Sneakers are the epitome of casual comfort. With their athletic appeal and cushioned support, sneakers have become a fashion staple, seamlessly blending with everyday wear or elevating a sporty-chic look. On the other hand, heels exude elegance and sophistication, adding a touch of glamour to any outfit. Whether you're stepping out for a night on the town or dressing up for a special event, heels provide an instant boost of confidence and style. From the laid-back vibe of sneakers to the refined allure of heels, both choices bring their unique charm to the world of fashion, catering to diverse tastes and preferences..
